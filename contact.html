Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Youth TV, our content, and how to get involved.

How can I appear as a guest on Youth TV?

We're always looking for inspiring young people to feature on our programs! Please send an email to guests@youthtvonline.com with a brief introduction about yourself, your expertise or story, and why you'd be a great fit for our audience. Our team reviews all submissions and will contact you if we think you'd be a good match for an upcoming episode.

Can Youth TV cover our youth event?

We love highlighting youth-focused events! Please submit your event details at least 4 weeks in advance to events@youthtvonline.com. Include the event name, date, location, purpose, expected attendance, and any special guests or activities. Our editorial team reviews all requests and will contact you if we're able to cover your event.

How do I submit content ideas or story pitches?

We welcome story ideas from our community! Send your pitch to pitches@youthtvonline.com with "STORY PITCH" in the subject line. Include a brief summary of your idea, why it's relevant to our audience, any potential sources or experts, and your contact information. Please note that while we review all submissions, we receive many pitches and may not be able to respond to each one individually.

What internship opportunities are available?

Youth TV offers internships in production, journalism, social media, marketing, and event planning. Internships are typically 3-6 months and open to students and recent graduates. Visit our Careers page for current opportunities and application details. We announce new internship positions at the beginning of each academic semester.

How can my organization partner with Youth TV?

We're always interested in exploring partnerships with organizations that align with our mission to empower youth. Please send partnership inquiries to partnerships@youthtvonline.com with information about your organization, proposed collaboration ideas, and how we can work together to benefit young people. Our partnership team will review your proposal and schedule a meeting if there's potential for collaboration.

Still have questions? We're here to help!
