Our Mission & Vision

We're on a journey to create a world where every young person has the tools, resources, and platform to thrive.

Our Mission

To provide a dynamic platform that empowers youth through engaging content, educational resources, and opportunities for personal and professional development.

  • Create accessible, youth-focused content
  • Amplify diverse young voices
  • Foster innovation and critical thinking

Our Vision

To become the leading global platform where every young person can access the knowledge, inspiration, and opportunities they need to shape a better future.

  • Reach 5 million youth by 2026
  • Establish mentorship programs in the major regions
  • Create a global network of young innovators

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to a platform reaching millions of young people worldwide

2015

Founded in a Dorm Room

Youth TV began as a passion project by university students who wanted to create content that resonated with young people.

2017

First Major Partnership

Secured our first corporate sponsorship, allowing us to expand our team and content offerings.

2019

Reached 1 Million Subscribers

Our YouTube channel hit a major milestone, confirming the need for youth-focused media.

2021

Global Expansion

Launched regional editions in 5 countries and established our mentorship program.

2023

Award-Winning Platform

Recognized as "Best Youth Media Platform" with over 5 million monthly active users.
